Below please find a resource center for brands in their earliest phases. This resource was born as I was
working to summarize answers to all my most frequently asked questions. The goal of this resource is to
uplift more than a few. I will continue to open doors for those that come from the fringe and help them
be awarded opportunities usually left for the center.
This continued commitment is supported by the “Post-Modern” Scholarship foundation, with its mission
statement being to supply black students an education in fashion via fundraising and mentorship in
partnership with the Fashion Scholarship Foundation.
